Who We Are

Built on Care, Grown by Community

Rebeka's Hair Design is a family-oriented salon dedicated to helping every client feel welcome, confident, and beautiful. From the moment you walk through our doors, our goal is to create a comfortable and friendly environment where you can relax and enjoy personalized care.

With over 20 years of experience, Rebeka's Hair Design has proudly served the Pittsburg, California community, providing professional hair services backed by expertise, creativity, and attention to detail. We take pride in listening to our clients' needs and delivering results that leave you feeling your best — inside and out.

Whether you're visiting for a simple refresh or a complete transformation, we are committed to ensuring you leave happy, confident, and satisfied with the services you receive.

Meet the Owner

Rebeka

For over 20 years, Rebeka has dedicated her career to making people look and feel their best. What began as a passion for hair and beauty grew into a salon built on a simple principle: every person who walks through the door deserves genuine care, honest attention, and results they can be proud of.

As both the owner and lead stylist, Rebeka brings a hands-on approach to every aspect of the salon. She's as invested in creating a warm, welcoming atmosphere as she is in perfecting a cut or color. Her clients trust her because she listens — really listens — and delivers with consistency and skill that comes from two decades of experience.

Rebeka's Hair Design reflects her values: professionalism without pretension, quality without compromise, and a personal touch that turns first-time visitors into long-term clients. Whether you speak English or Spanish, you'll feel at home the moment you arrive.
